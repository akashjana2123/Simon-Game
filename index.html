Press A Key to Start

Rules...

1. One out of four cards will be highlighted, and a sound will be played. You have to press the highlighted card.
2. If one out of four cards is highlighted, this time you need to press the first card, and then the highlighted card.
3. Again, the same procedure applies, where you need to click the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and so on.
4. If you press the wrong card, the game will stop.
